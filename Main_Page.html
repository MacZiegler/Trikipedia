Other areas of Trikipedia

  • Community portal – Bulletin board, projects, resources and activities which would cover a wide range of Trikipedia areas.

  • Local embassy – For Trikipedia-related communication in languages other than English.
  • Reference desk – Serving as a virtual librarian, the Trikipedia volunteer tackles your questions on a wide range of subjects.
  • Site news – Announcements, updates, articles and press releases on Trikipedia.
  • Village pump – For discussions about Trikipedia itself, including areas for technical issues and policies.
